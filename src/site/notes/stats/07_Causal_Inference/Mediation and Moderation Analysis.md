## Definition

> [!abstract] Overview
> **Mediation and Moderation Analysis** are statistical methods used to understand "how" or "when" effects occur.
>
> *   **Mediation:** Explains the mechanism (X -> M -> Y).
> *   **Moderation:** Explains the context (Effect of X on Y depends on Z).
