## Definition

> [!abstract] Overview
> **Sampling Bias** occurs when some members of the intended population are less likely to be included than others, resulting in a non-representative sample.

---

## Purpose

- To ensure the validity of statistical inferences about a population.
- To identify and mitigate sources of systematic error in survey design and data collection.
