## Definition

> [!abstract] Overview
> **Anomaly Detection** (or Outlier Detection) is the identification of rare items, events, or observations which raise suspicions by differing significantly from the majority of the data.

## Common Techniques
*   [[stats/01_Foundations/Z-Scores\|Z-Scores]] for univariate normals.
*   [[stats/05_Unsupervised_Learning/Isolation Forest\|Isolation Forest]].
*   [[stats/05_Unsupervised_Learning/DBSCAN\|DBSCAN]].
*   **Autoencoders** (Deep Learning).
